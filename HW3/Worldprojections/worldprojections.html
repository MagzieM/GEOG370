In this project I learned how to analyze the unique presentation and purpose of various projections.

I used QGIS mapping software to display different projections of the world map.

There are 4 main components of a spherical map: Area, shape, distance, direction. When projected onto a 2D surface, some of these components must be sacrificed. Different projections attempt to preserve different components based on the planned use of the map. For instance, for navigation, distance and direction are usually the most important components. Each of the 9 projections below were made with different goals for preserving different map components.
