Georeferencing 1972 Aerial Image of Bethlehem PA

Georeferenced Map

This aerial image from 1972 shows Bethlehem, PA streets as they were the year my grandfather taught my mother to drive. This was the year she learned she was night-blind as she drove her car into a neighbor's front lawn. Unfortunately the address of this incident was lost to time. The 2015 image shows how downtown Bethlehem has changed over the last 40+ years.
